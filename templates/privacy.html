Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 21 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you register, we collect:

  • Full Name
  • EVSU ID Number
  • EVSU Email Address
  • Contact Number
  • Department
  • Password (hashed)

2. How Your Data Is Used

Your information is used to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Verify your EVSU identity
  • Process parking sticker applications
  • Support campus safety and security

3. Data Protection

We secure your data through password hashing, secured databases, and limited administrative access. We do not sell or share your information to third parties.

4. User Rights

You may request to update or delete your account or request a copy of your stored data.

5. Cookies and Logs

The system may collect basic information such as IP address, login time, and browser type for security purposes.

6. Data Sharing

We may share limited information only with EVSU Security Office and authorized staff for verification and safety.

7. Data Retention

Your data is stored as long as the account is active or up to 2 years after inactivity unless deletion is requested.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy. Notifications will be posted on the system.

9. Contact Information

For privacy concerns contact:
EVSU Parking Management Office
Email: evsuparking@evsu.edu.ph
Office Hours: Mon – Fri, 8AM – 5PM
